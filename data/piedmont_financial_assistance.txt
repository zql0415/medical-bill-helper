Source: Piedmont Healthcare - Financial Assistance Policy
URL: https://www.piedmont.org/patients-and-visitors/billing-and-financial-resources

ELIGIBILITY
Piedmont Healthcare (PHC) offers financial assistance to uninsured patients who are Georgia residents with gross household income at or below 300% of the Federal Poverty Level (FPL). Qualifying patients receive a 100% discount on medically necessary care. PHC does not consider race, gender, age, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, or immigrant status when determining eligibility.

WHAT IS COVERED
- Emergency medical services in an emergency room
- Non-elective services for life-threatening circumstances
- Other medically necessary services
- Does NOT cover elective or cosmetic procedures

HOW TO APPLY
1. Online at piedmont.org
2. In person at any Piedmont Hospital admissions/registration area
3. By phone: call Customer Solutions Center at 855-788-1212
4. By mail: Customer Solutions Center, Piedmont Healthcare, P.O. Box 571153, Atlanta, GA 30357

EMAIL: assistance@piedmont.org

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
- Proof of identity
- Proof of Georgia residency
- Proof of household income (pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements)
- Proof of household size

APPROVAL TIMEFRAME
- Approved assistance covers an 8-month period
- PHC will not send approved accounts to collections or report to credit bureaus during this period
- No wage garnishment or liens on primary residence before financial assistance determination

ADDITIONAL OPTIONS IF YOU DON'T QUALIFY
- Uninsured discounts: automatic discounts for patients without insurance
- Hardship assistance: for insured and uninsured patients with financial difficulties
- Payment plans: interest-free, call 855-788-1212
