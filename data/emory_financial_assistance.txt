ELIGIBILITY
Emory Healthcare offers financial assistance to uninsured and underinsured patients for emergency or medically necessary care. Eligibility is based on U.S. Federal Poverty Guidelines:
- Uninsured patients may qualify if income is up to 350% of the federal poverty level.
- Underinsured patients may qualify if income is up to 350% of the federal poverty level.
